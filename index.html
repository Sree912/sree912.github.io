About Our Doctor

Meet Dr. Sarath Babu, MD

Dr. Sarath Babu is a highly experienced physician specialising in Internal Medicine and Addiction Medicine. With over four decades of dedicated practice, Dr. Babu brings deep clinical expertise and a patient-first approach to every consultation at MedCenter.

He is fluent in Hindi, Urdu, Persian, Telugu, and English, ensuring every patient can communicate comfortably in their preferred language. Dr. Babu offers both in-person and telehealth appointments and is currently welcoming new patients at MedCenter.

Immunisations

Vaccinations Available at MedCenter

All CDC-recommended adult vaccines are administered on-premises by Dr. Babu's team. No pharmacy trip needed.

🍂

Influenza (Flu Shot)

Annual Flu Vaccine Recommended every year for all adults. Protects against seasonal influenza viruses. Available each flu season — call to confirm current availability.
💉

Tdap (Tetanus, Diphtheria, Pertussis)

Tdap Booster One Tdap dose for adults who have not previously received it; Td booster every 10 years thereafter. Recommended during each pregnancy.
💡
MedCenter administers Flu and Tdap vaccines on-premises — no pharmacy trip needed. Vaccines are typically covered by Medicare and major insurance plans. Call (732) 390-0028 to confirm availability and book your vaccine appointment.
